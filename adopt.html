Adoptions

We operate as a foster home for Gutter Kitties and 9 Lives Orphanage, fostering their cats in a dedicated space. We act on behalf of these rescues, helping to facilitate the cats' adoptions when they are ready to move on to forever homes, with 100% of the adoption fees going back to the rescue.
