About Us

Purrs & Beans (formerly BaristaCats Cafe in Auckland CBD) is New Zealand's longest running cat cafe, now located on 188 Hinemoa Street, Birkenhead, in the heart of the "village with a view".

We are a small, independently run, social enterprise and sanctuary to a clowder of very special resident rescue cats, all of whom are older and some with special needs. Because of this, admission into the cat area is restricted to 10+ as part of our SPCA welfare plan which we enforce strictly to ensure the safety and welfare of our cats.

To spend time with our fluffy gang, we do have an entry fee in place which contributes towards the cats' care and helps us continue to run the premises which is their forever home. With limited capacity in each session so as not to overcrowd the cat area and overwhelm our cats, we strongly recommend booking online to guarantee your spot.
