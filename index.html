There is an admission fee to spend time with our cats and entry is at set times and we limit the number of people in each session so booking in advance is highly recommended. All bookings and purchases contribute towards the cats' care and continual running of the premise which is their home.

What we're all about

We are a small, independently run, social enterprise and sanctuary to a clowder of very special resident rescue cats, all of whom are older and some with special needs.

We partner with multiple rescues and have a dedicated adoption room on site with rescue cats waiting to meet their forever families.

It is our goal to provide an enjoyable and enriching space for both humans and cats alike where we hope our guests not only have a purrific time but learn a little too.
